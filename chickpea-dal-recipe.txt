rinse 4 tins of white chickpeas + 1 of brown and put in large pan with water, salt and tumeric and boil

chop 5 onions, 4 chillies, 1 garlic clove and fry with cumin seeds and paprika until transparent.

add tin of chopped tomatoes to the onions etc and fry with tumeric and salt and cardamon seeds + methi for ~15 mins

(can also add garam masala here...)

add the fried stuff to the chickpeas and stir (should be thick)

add chopped coriander, stir and take off the heat to stand for 5mins before serving with rice.
